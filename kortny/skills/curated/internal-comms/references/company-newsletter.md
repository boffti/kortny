# Company Newsletter

Vendored from anthropics/skills@57546260 under Apache-2.0, adapted for Slack delivery.

---

## Purpose

A company-wide newsletter summarises the past week or month for the entire organisation. It is sent via Slack (and optionally email), so it must be consumable in a scan — roughly 20–25 bullet points organised by theme.

---

## Voice and tone

- Write in "we" — you are part of the company.
- Each bullet is 1–2 sentences, no longer.
- Link generously: documents with wide readership, prominent Slack posts, announcements from leadership.

---

## Information gathering

Pull from available sources when connected tools allow:
- **Slack**: posts in large channels with strong engagement (reactions, thread replies) — especially announcements from leadership
- **Documents**: widely-viewed docs from the period — vision docs, quarterly plans, strategy updates, authored by senior contributors
- **Calendar**: large-audience meetings (all-hands, company reviews, board updates); attached documents from those meetings are high-signal
- **External press**: any notable coverage of the company in the period

If tools are unavailable, ask the user what they want to cover. They may paste the content directly and need it organised and polished into this format.

---

## Sections

Break the newsletter into 3–5 thematic clusters. Examples of how to cluster:

- Product · Go-to-market · Finance
- External news · Internal news
- Execution · Recruiting · Vision

Pick the clusters that fit what actually happened that week — don't force every section if a category had nothing notable.

---

## Prioritisation

**Include:**
- Company-wide announcements from leadership
- Major milestones and launches
- Information that affects most employees
- External recognition or press

**Exclude:**
- Team-specific updates that belong in a 3P
- Information relevant to only a small group
- Anything that was already communicated in a separate company-wide message this period

---

## Format template

```
:megaphone: Company Announcements
- [Announcement 1]
- [Announcement 2]

:dart: Progress on Priorities
- [Area 1]
  - [Sub-point]
  - [Sub-point]
- [Area 2]
  - [Sub-point]

:people_holding_hands: People & Culture
- [Update]

:thread: Notable Slack Posts
- [Linked post summary]
```

Adapt the section headers and emoji to fit the week. The template is a starting point, not a rigid structure.

---

## Delivery

Post to the main Slack announcement channel. For longer newsletters, offer to create a canvas version as the authoritative record, with a shorter summary post in the channel that links to it. If this newsletter recurs, note: "this can be paired with a recurring schedule — ask me to set that up."
