## Custom theme generation

If none of the 10 pre-built themes fit, generate a custom theme. Based on the user's brief (brand colors, mood/adjectives, medium), construct:
1. A primary palette of 4–6 named hex values
2. Typography pairing (heading face + body face)
3. A name that captures the aesthetic (e.g. "Industrial Calm", "Boreal Dusk")

Check contrast: primary color on white must meet WCAG AA (4.5:1 for body text). If it fails, adjust toward compliance and say so.
