# Marketing Metrics Reference

## Acquisition cost
- **CAC** = Total marketing + sales spend in period / New customers acquired in period
- **Blended CAC** = All channels combined. **Channel CAC** = spend on that channel / customers attributed to it.
- **CAC payback period** = CAC / (Monthly recurring revenue per customer × Gross margin %)

## Return on investment
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend) = Revenue attributed / Ad spend. Healthy: depends on margins; 3x is a common floor for ecommerce.
- **Marketing ROI** = (Revenue attributed − Marketing cost) / Marketing cost × 100

## Lifetime value
- **LTV** = Average revenue per customer × Gross margin % × Average customer lifetime (months)
- **LTV:CAC ratio** — healthy B2B SaaS: ≥3:1. Below 1:1 means you're spending more to acquire than you'll ever recoup.

## Funnel metrics
- **MQL rate** = MQLs / Total leads
- **MQL→SQL rate** = SQLs / MQLs
- **Conversion rate (web)** = Goal completions / Sessions

## Content / SEO
- **Organic traffic MoM growth** = (This month sessions − Last month sessions) / Last month sessions
- **Branded vs. non-branded split**: flag if branded search dominates (awareness vs. intent)

## Email
- **Open rate**: industry average varies widely; use your own baseline as the reference.
- **Click-to-open rate (CTOR)** = Clicks / Opens (better signal than raw click rate)
- **Unsubscribe rate**: >0.5% per send is a list-health warning.

## Attribution models (choose one, state it)
| Model | Logic | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Last touch | All credit to last channel before conversion | Bottom-funnel optimization |
| First touch | All credit to first touchpoint | Awareness investment decisions |
| Linear | Equal credit to all touchpoints | Full-funnel view |
| Time decay | More credit to recent touches | Short sales cycles |
