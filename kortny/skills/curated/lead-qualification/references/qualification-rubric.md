# Lead Qualification Rubric

## BANT scoring (simple framework, 100 points)

### Budget (25 points)
| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Budget confirmed and allocated | 25 |
| Budget likely — company size / stage fits typical buyer | 15 |
| Budget unclear — small company, no signals | 5 |
| Explicitly no budget | 0 |

### Authority (25 points)
| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Economic buyer or C-suite directly engaged | 25 |
| Director or VP — likely influencer, may not own budget | 18 |
| Manager-level — champion, not economic buyer | 10 |
| IC — needs to escalate before any decision | 3 |

### Need (30 points)
| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Described specific pain tied to your product's core value | 30 |
| Vague pain that maps to your product | 18 |
| Browsing / exploratory — no stated problem | 8 |
| No relevant need stated | 0 |

### Timeline (20 points)
| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Active evaluation, decision in <30 days | 20 |
| Decision in 30-90 days | 14 |
| "Looking at options" — 3-6 months | 7 |
| No timeline | 3 |

## Scoring guide
- **70-100**: Qualified — route to AE.
- **40-69**: Nurture — what would move them to 70+?
- **0-39**: Disqualify — note reason (bad fit vs. bad timing).

## ICP fit bonus / penalty (apply after BANT)
- **+10**: Industry and company size in ICP sweet spot.
- **+5**: Known buying trigger present (recent funding, new hire in role).
- **−10**: Explicitly out-of-ICP (excluded vertical, too small, wrong geography).
- **−15**: Competitor-locked with long contract stated.

## MEDDIC reference (for complex sales)
| Factor | What to confirm |
|---|---|
| Metrics | What measurable outcome does the buyer expect? |
| Economic Buyer | Who owns the budget and signs the contract? |
| Decision Criteria | What are their explicit evaluation criteria? |
| Decision Process | What steps must happen before a contract is signed? |
| Identify Pain | What's the cost of not solving this? |
| Champion | Who inside the org is actively selling for you? |
