# Brand compliance checklist

Use this checklist to audit content against a brand's guidelines.

## Voice and tone

- [ ] Tone matches the brand's formality level (check known workspace facts or brief)
- [ ] No prohibited words or phrases (check brand vocabulary list if present)
- [ ] Active voice used throughout (passive voice should be < 20% of sentences)
- [ ] Consistent person (first person "we" vs third person brand name — pick one per piece)
- [ ] Filler openers removed ("I'm excited to share…" etc.)
- [ ] No corporate jargon unless the brand explicitly uses it

## Visual identity (for designed artifacts)

- [ ] Brand colors used from the approved palette; no off-palette colors
- [ ] Logo present where required; clear space respected
- [ ] Typography: correct typefaces used; no substitutions
- [ ] No competitor logos, colors, or trademarks

## Messaging

- [ ] Core value proposition stated or implied (does the piece reflect what the brand stands for?)
- [ ] Claims are accurate and can be substantiated
- [ ] No pricing or feature claims without verification
- [ ] Legal / compliance terms included where required (varies by industry)

## Audience fit

- [ ] Language matches the target audience's vocabulary
- [ ] Reading level appropriate (use the Flesch-Kincaid check mentally: shorter sentences = easier)
- [ ] No assumed knowledge that the audience wouldn't have

## Rating scale

For each failing check, assign severity:
- **High** — fix before publish (incorrect facts, wrong brand colors, prohibited claims)
- **Medium** — fix if time allows (tone drift, overuse of jargon)
- **Low** — nice to have (minor phrasing preferences)
