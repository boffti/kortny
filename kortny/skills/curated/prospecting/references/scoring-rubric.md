# Prospect Scoring Rubric

Score each prospect out of 10. Add points from each category:

## Fit (0-5 points)
| Criterion | Points |
|---|---|
| Industry matches ICP vertical | +2 |
| Company size in target band | +2 |
| Geography matches | +1 |

## Signal strength (0-5 points)
| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Recent funding (≤90 days) | +2 |
| Hiring in buyer role or adjacent function | +2 |
| Job posting mentions a tool you replace | +3 |
| Product launch or company announcement | +1 |
| Tech stack match (uses integrations you support) | +1 |

## Scoring guide
- **8-10**: High priority — personalize outreach immediately.
- **5-7**: Medium priority — include in next sequence.
- **3-4**: Low priority — nurture or deprioritize.
- **0-2**: Discard — does not meet minimum fit.

Note: Points cap at 10. A single strong signal (job posting replacing your tool) can push a borderline-fit company to medium priority.
