# Comparison Page Structure

## [Your Product] vs [Competitor] — [Year]

### Hero (above the fold)
- Headline: "[Your Product] vs [Competitor]: [core differentiator in plain English]"
- Subheadline: one sentence on who this page is for and what they'll learn.

### Quick verdict (2-3 sentences)
State the conclusion upfront. Who wins, for whom, and on what dimensions.

### Feature comparison table
| Feature | [Your Product] | [Competitor] |

### Where [Your Product] wins
3-4 bullets. Specific, factual, with numbers where available.

### Where [Competitor] might be the better fit
1-2 honest use cases where the competitor is genuinely stronger. This builds trust.
